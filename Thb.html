Once upon a time, there was a bird that fell into a mountain cave and needed to get out of there. Jump!!!, climb!!!, avoid enemies!!! and get out of the mountain in this platform game. #adventure #retro #platformer #strategy
Our character is a fallen bird, his wing is hurt and he needs to leave. He can jump but not too much. Help him get out of there as soon as possible.
There are many types of platforms to get out of there, many of them fall, others stay, others explode and others burn, you must discover each function of the platforms, just as there are dangerous enemies and colossal monsters.
